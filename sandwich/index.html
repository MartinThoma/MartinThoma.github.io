Sandwich

Contents

  • Ingredients
  • Tools
  • Preparation

I eat sandwiches a lot. I make them myself.

Ingredients

For two sandwiches (about 4 sandwiches are enough for dinner for one person):

  • Toast
  • 2 sliced sausage - there are vegetarian options at Aldi which taste similar as well
  • Some table mustard
  • Some Balsamic Cream Vinegar
  • 1 Tomato
  • 4 Small pickled cucumbers
  • Some french fried onions
  • Some cheese (I like Emmental cheese)

Additionally, if you like it:

  • Salad
  • Olives

Tools

  • A Toaster

Preparation

For all of the following, you will need roughly 5 minutes:

  1. Toast the toast
  2. Cut the Tomato and the pickle into slices
  3. Put cream cheese on the toast
  4. Put some mustard and balsamic cream on the toast and add the french fired onions
  5. Put the sausage slice on the toast
  6. Put the cheese on the toast
  7. Put the tomato and the pickled cucumber on the toast
  8. Put some mustard / balsamic cream on top
