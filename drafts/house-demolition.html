House Demolition

Contents

  • How it works
    • Preparation
  • Cost
  • Footnotes

I'm interested in house building for quite a while now. I want to live in my own house at some point and there are many options to get there. One of them is to buy an old house in a nice location, tear it down and build a new one. But how expensive is that and how does it work?

How it works

Preparation

  • Remove furniture, make all rooms empty (especially cellar / attic)
  • Remove doors
  • Remove insulation
  • Remove everything made out of wood

Cost

According to 1, is about 10 000 EUR to 25 000 EUR:

Price Position Comment
1000 Planning
2000 Preparation Remove interiour and possibly pollutants like Asbestos. If pollutants are present, it becomes more expensive
10,000 Demolition Assuming it is easy to access and it can be demolitioned. More expensive if it needs to be deconstructed.
4,000 Cellar
3,000 Waste dumping
