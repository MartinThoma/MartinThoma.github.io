Action Cams

Contents

  • Products

Action Cams are small, robust cameras which are often used for sports. They are often mounted on helmets, bikes, or cars. The most famous brand is GoPro.

Categories to compare them are:

  • Video quality (resolution, frame rate)
  • Image quality (resolution, sensor size)
  • Battery life
  • Size / Weight
  • Price
  • Accessories (waterproof case, mounts)
  • Connectivity (WiFi, Bluetooth)
  • GPS
  • Display
  • Microphone
  • Storage
  • Remote control
  • Software
