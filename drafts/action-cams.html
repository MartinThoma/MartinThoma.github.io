Action Cams

Contents

  • Products

Action cameras are small, robust cameras often used for sports activities. They are frequently mounted on helmets, bikes, or cars. GoPro is the most well-known brand in this category.

Key features to compare when evaluating action cameras:

  • Video quality (resolution, frame rate)
  • Image quality (resolution, sensor size)
  • Battery life
  • Size and weight
  • Price
  • Available accessories (waterproof case, mounts)
  • Connectivity options (WiFi, Bluetooth)
  • GPS capability
  • Display quality
  • Microphone quality
  • Storage capacity
  • Remote control functionality
  • Software ecosystem
