Ikea Pax

Contents

  • What I like
  • What I miss
  • Assembling

Ikeas Pax system is a modular wardrobe system. I bought:

  • 901.839.91: PAX Korpus Kleiderschrank: 236cm x 50cm x 58cm (H x W x D)
  • 902.568.93: Komplement Kleiderstange
  • 502.463.06: Komplement Schublade
  • 702.779.81: Komplement Boden

It was nice to combine with MALM:

  • 203.598.23: Schreibtisch, Eichenfurnier weiß lasiert
  • 091.750.57: Bettgestell hoch
  • 702.787.06: Lönset Federholzrahmen

What I like

  • All manuals are online as PDF. You just need to remember the article numbers
  • You can combine things to what you need.

What I miss

  • If you choose to have doors, there are 18cm until the first drawer can beginn. There seem not to be any Ikea boxes of the necessary size.
  • You can choose to make the front at the bottom of the Korpus either relative directly at the front or about 1cm more to the inner. I now have two Korpus where I choose one depth on one side and the other depth on the other side. Sometimes less choice is better.

Assembling

I missed one hint when assembling the cabinet: Use many nails for the back of it! When you use too few, things can fall behind.
