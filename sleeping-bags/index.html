Sleeping Bags

I have a moorhead micro lite III 185 sleeping bag. It weights about 830g, is filled with TECMOORFILL (100% polyester) and has a comfort temperature of +9°C, limit of +5°C and extreme of -8°C (ISO EN 23537:2016). It is about 214x78cm big and packs to roughly a cylinder of height 33cm and diameter 18cm.

I need a sleeping bag with comfort temperature below 0°C.

Features

  • 3 Zippers: You can create an opening, e.g. for your arms
  • Compression Sack
