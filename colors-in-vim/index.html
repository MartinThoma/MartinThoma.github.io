Colorize Vim

You can highlight your current line by adding the following line to your .vimrc: hi CursorLine cterm=NONE ctermbg=187

The following line will use color 0 (black) for the text color of the line numbers and color 187 (see above) for the background. highlight LineNr ctermfg=0 ctermbg=187
