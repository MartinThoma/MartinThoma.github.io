This is an article I had for quite a while as a draft. As part of my yearly cleanup, I've published it without finishing it. It might not be finished or have other problems.

I don't want to spend a lot of thought into what I'm going to eat. I'm completely fine with eating cereals and bread for many weeks, but my girlfriend wants to eat something warm twice a day. In order to simplify my live, I had the idea to create a yearly plan. 365 different main dishes.
